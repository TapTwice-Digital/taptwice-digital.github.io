Arvio Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

Overview

Arvio is a personal budgeting app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

Data Storage

All of your financial data — transactions, budgets, categories, and settings — is stored locally on your device. If you are signed into iCloud, your data is automatically synced to your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is only accessible to you and anyone you explicitly choose to share a ledger with.

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple manages the storage and encryption.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.
  • We do not have servers that store your information.

Camera & Photo Library

Arvio may request access to your camera or photo library to let you attach receipt photos to transactions. These images are stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. They are never uploaded to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Arvio uses logo.dev to display company logos for recurring transactions. When a logo is requested, the company domain name is sent to the logo.dev API. No personal or financial data is included in these requests. Logos are cached locally on your device.

iCloud Sharing

Arvio supports sharing a ledger with other people using Apple's iCloud sharing. When you share a ledger, the participants you invite can see the transactions and budgets within that shared ledger. Sharing is managed entirely through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Arvio does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at ben@arvio.app.
